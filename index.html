Our Story

A ranch that breathes
with the land

Loisoi Ranch and Resort sits on 50 acres of untouched highland wilderness in Nanyuki, at the foothills of Mount Kenya. Our traditional thatched cottages and unique rock cave suite are hidden within lush bush.

We blend the heritage of a Kenyan ranch with the warmth of modern luxury — for guests who want more than just a bed.
